Brownies

  • Time: ~1 Hour
  • Serves: ~16
Ingredients Quantity
Granulated sugar 1 1/2 cups
All-Purpose Flour 3/4 cup
Cocoa Powder 2/3 cup
Powdered Sugar 1/2 cup
Dark Chocolate Chips 1/2 cup
Sea Salt 3/4 teaspoon
Large Eggs 2
Canola oil or extra-virgin olive oil 1/2 cup
Water 2 tablespoons
Vanilla 1/2 tablespoon

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 325Â°F. Lightly spray an 8x8 baking dish (not a 9x9 dish or your brownies will overcook) with cooking spray and line it with parchment paper. Spray the parchment paper.

Step 2:In a medium bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, chocolate chips, and salt.

Step 3:In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, olive oil, water, and vanilla.

Step 4: Sprinkle the dry mix over the wet mix and stir until just combined.

Step 3:Pour the batter into the prepared pan (it'll be thick - that's ok) and use a spatula to smooth the top. Bake for 40 to 48 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out with only a few crumbs attached (note: it's better to pull the brownies out early than to leave them in too long). Cool completely before slicing.*** Store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. These also freeze well!
